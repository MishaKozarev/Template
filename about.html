FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I customize my pizza?
+

You can customize your pizza by selecting your preferred toppings and crust type during the ordering process. We offer a wide range of toppings and crust options to suit your taste.

Do you offer gluten-free pizza?
+

Yes, we offer gluten-free pizza options. Simply select the gluten-free crust option when placing your order, and we'll ensure your pizza is prepared accordingly to meet your dietary needs.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and online payment platforms such as PayPal. You can choose your preferred payment method during the checkout process.

What is your delivery area?
+

You can check if we deliver to your location by entering your address during the ordering process.

What are your delivery hours?
+

Our delivery hours are from 8:00 to 22:00. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on the day of the week.

Do you offer catering services?
+

Yes, we offer catering services for events and gatherings of all sizes. Please contact our catering team for more information and to discuss your specific requirements.

Can I track my delivery?
+

Yes, you can track your delivery in real-time using our online tracking system. Once your order is placed, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS.

Do you offer vegan options?
+

Yes, we offer a variety of vegan options on our menu, including vegan pizzas and sides. Look for the "vegan" label next to the menu items when ordering.
