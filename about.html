Behind the Flavors: Our Passionate Team

We followed our heart and it led us to cooking

At Tasty, we believe that the secret ingredient to any great dish isn't just the fresh produce or the rich spices; it's the people who make it all come together. Our team is a vibrant tapestry of culinary experts, food enthusiasts, and service-focused individuals, each bringing their unique flavors to the table.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in the vibrant city of Austin, Texas, Tasty embarked on its journey with one clear mission in mind: to revolutionize the way people experience food delivery.

Fueled by a passion for culinary excellence and a commitment to convenience, we set out to create a platform where anyone could enjoy their favorite meals, from savory pizzas to gourmet burgers, without ever having to leave the comfort of their home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and convenience to the dining table. We aim to make gourmet dining accessible to everyone, everywhere, by offering a seamless online ordering system, fast delivery, and unparalleled customer service.

We believe that everyone deserves to indulge in delicious food, and we're here to make that happen, one delivery at a time.

FAQ

The most common questions

How do I place an order?


Placing an order is easy! Simply visit our website, choose your favorite items from our menu, and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery information, and confirm your order. You'll receive an email confirmation with your order details.

Can I customize my pizza or burger?


Absolutely! We understand that everyone has unique tastes. When you select a pizza or burger on our menu, you'll have the option to add or remove ingredients as you like. Just look for the "Customize" button before adding the item to your cart.

What are your delivery hours?


Our delivery hours are from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, seven days a week. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on your location and order volume.

Do you offer any vegetarian or vegan options?


Yes, we do! We're proud to offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options across our menu. Look for the "Vegetarian" and "Vegan" labels next to the dish names to find our plant-based offerings.

How can I track my order?


Once your order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can click on this link at any time to see the status of your delivery. Additionally, our delivery team will send you updates via SMS as your order gets closer.

What should I do if I have an issue with my order?


If there's any issue with your order, we're here to help make it right. Please contact us as soon as possible at +1 212 425 8617 or via email at INFORMATION@OFFICE.COM. Our customer service team will work swiftly to resolve any problems to ensure your satisfaction.

Can I schedule my delivery for a later time or date?


Yes, you can! During the checkout process, you'll have the option to select a delivery date and time that best fits your schedule. We recommend placing your order in advance to ensure your preferred delivery slot is available.

Do you offer catering services for events?


We do offer catering services for a variety of events, including parties, business meetings, and special occasions. You can choose from our catering menu options or contact us to customize a menu that fits your event's needs.
