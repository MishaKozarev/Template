Common Questions

Get answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about our yoga studio.

Contact Us
What equipment do I need to start yoga?
+

To begin practicing yoga, you'll need a comfortable yoga mat, suitable clothing, and an open mind ready for new experiences.

Is previous yoga experience required?
+

No, our classes cater to all levels, including beginners. Our instructors will guide you through each session, regardless of your experience.

Do I need to reserve a spot in advance?
+

It's recommended to book your spot in advance, especially for popular classes, to ensure availability and to streamline your experience.

What is the duration of each yoga class?
+

The duration of our yoga classes varies, typically ranging from 60 to 90 minutes, depending on the style and intensity of the session.

Can I attend classes if I have health concerns?
+

We encourage you to consult with your healthcare provider before starting any exercise program, including yoga, especially if you have health concerns or medical conditions.
