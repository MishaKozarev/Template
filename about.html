Established

John is the mastermind behind Lather Lux. With a background in chemistry and a passion for skincare, he founded Lather Lux to provide premium quality soap products that nourish and pamper the skin. His dedication to using natural ingredients and innovative formulations has propelled Lather Lux to the forefront of the soap industry, offering customers luxurious and effective cleansing experiences.

John's vision for Lather Lux is to inspire individuals to prioritize self-care while being mindful of environmental impact, fostering a harmonious relationship between people and nature.
