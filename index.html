The Founder of Office Craft.

As the visionary founder of Office Craft, Rajesh Patel brings a wealth of experience, expertise, and passion to the world of office furniture manufacturing. With 36 years of experience in the industry, Rajesh Patel is known for his innovative furniture designs, attention to detail, and commitment to quality.

Driven by a deep-seated passion for creating inspiring workspaces that enhance productivity and comfort, Rajesh Patel has played a pivotal role in shaping Office Craft's identity.

He is actively involved in mentorship programs and industry associations, demonstrating his commitment to excellence and leadership in the field of office furniture manufacturing.

Rajesh Patel's creative vision and hands-on approach to design have been instrumental in the success of numerous projects, from ergonomic chairs to modular workstations, providing functional and aesthetically pleasing furniture solutions for offices across India.
