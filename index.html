Marvin McKinney

The Founder of Arocen.

As the visionary founder of Arocen, Marvin McKinney brings a wealth of experience, expertise, and passion to the world of architecture. With 36 Years of experience in the industry, Marvin McKinney is known for their innovative design solutions, keen eye for detail, and commitment to excellence.

Driven by a deep-seated passion for creating inspiring spaces that enrich lives, Marvin McKinney has played a pivotal role in shaping the firm's identity.

They are actively involved in mentorship programs & industry associations, demonstrating their commitment to excellence and leadership in the field.

Marvin McKinney's creative vision and hands-on approach to design have been instrumental in the success of numerous projects, from residential homes to commercial developments and beyond.
