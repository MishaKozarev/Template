About Priya Devi

Meet Priya Devi, the visionary founder of Radiant Lotus Yoga Studio. Born and raised in the serene valleys of India, Priya's journey with yoga began at a young age, inspired by her grandmother's teachings. Determined to share the transformative power of yoga with the world, Priya embarked on a spiritual quest, traveling across continents to study under renowned gurus. Armed with ancient wisdom and modern insights, she established Radiant Lotus, a sanctuary where seekers find solace and strength. Priya's compassionate guidance and profound understanding of yoga philosophy have touched countless lives, illuminating the path to inner radiance and holistic well-being.
