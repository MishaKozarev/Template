Tailored to your needs

Yes, we know... it's easy to say it, but that's the fact. We did put a lot of thought into the template. Demure was designed by an award-winning designer. Layouts you will find in our template are custom made to fit the industry after carefully made research.

Optimized for efficiency

We used our best practices to make sure your new website loads fast. All of the images are compressed to have as little size as possible. Whenever possible we used vector formats - the format made for the web.

Responsive design

Demure Template is optimized to offer a frictionless experience on every screen. No matter how you combine our sections, they will look good on desktop, tablet, and phone.

Flexible and customizable

Both complex and simple animations are an inseparable element of modern website. We created our animations in a way that can be easily reused, even by Webflow beginners.

Modular setup

Our template is modular, meaning you can combine different sections as well as single elements, like buttons, images, etc. with each other without losing on consistency of the design. Long story short, different elements will always look good together.

100% adaptable

On top of being modular, Demure was created using the best Webflow techniques, like: global Color Swatches, reusable classes, symbols and more.

Integrated CMS

Demure includes a blog and careers collections that are made on the powerful Webflow CMS. This will let you add new content extremely easily.

Seamless ecommerce integration

Demure Startup & Tech Template comes with eCommerce set up, so you can start selling your services straight away.

Includes Figma files

For complete design control, you'll receive the Figma file along with your PrimeOffice package.
